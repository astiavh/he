Core Type Eight

Gut-Rejection

The Whale, Self-Ordained Autocrat, The Mountain

"#8 forms the point of fixation with power and autonomy in its purest form - separating the will of the self from all else and continuously expanding its reach."

Gut (8, 9, 1)

The stable point in the gut center.

Assertive (3, 7, 8)

Expanding reach to obtain goals.

Rejection (2, 5, 8)

Rejecting vulnerability and personal needs.

Reactive (4, 6, 8)

High-conflict, intensity-driven response.

The Mechanism of Eight

This type rejects the gut center’s need for tranquil consistency in favor of constant movement toward immediate impulses and protection of core boundaries. They assert willpower and physical control, fundamentally believing their own story as fact and trampling competing narratives seen as offenses.

Unlike 9 who withdraws, 8 finds it difficult to disconnect; the sense of self is tied to how one expands and controls boundaries externally. This creates a hardened impermeability in the gut intelligence center.

Key Qualities

  • — Unquestionable "largeness" felt in presence.
  • — Identifying false integrity in others and environment.
  • — Rejecting personal needs for protection in favor of toughness.
  • — Reveals hidden conflicts through generating overt conflict.
  • — Insensitivity to softer emotions as vulnerability is "kept for self."
  • — Brutish, retaliatory struggle with defeat.

The Will to Power

For the 8, the will to power is the will to live. Reality and its contingencies are merely means to an end. Admission of weakness is something "others do," while the 8 redirects attention to increasing capacity and control.

They replace the fear of vulnerability by guarding the softest part of the gut—the permeability of being—by becoming impenetrable. This reduces the gut's breadth of experience to a singular, instinctual command of strength.

8w7

The Unstoppable Force

Distrusts the gut’s efficacy in immediate reaction. Prioritizes future planning. Verbally assertive, bratty, and creative. Causes mental exhaustion in others.

8w9

The Immovable Object

Trusts the body's immediate response. Relaxes until action is necessary. Physically grounded, more non-verbal, and quietly "scary."

The Narrative

Constant climbing of the mountain. More presence. More real. More intense.

"At the top, I will be able to tend to the vulnerable, but until then, time isn't to be wasted on nuances of emotionality."

Core Type Nine

The Hypnagogue

The Empath, The Kaleidoscope, The Boundary Between Blindness and Enlightenment

"The point of attachment to the question of existence. Wavering between integrated presence and dissociation from control of boundaries and movement."

Gut (8, 9, 1)

Center of Energy that processes the quality of presence and being.

Withdrawn (9, 4, 5)

Removing selfhood from the world to protect transformation and harmony.

Attachment (9, 3, 6)

Identification with the merging point between people; hyper-individuation vs overadaptation.

The Mechanism of Nine

9 is fixated on identifying the right emotional place for the body and psyche. Boundaries, experiences, and connections are experienced as potential places of integration and/or confusion and decay. They prioritize the maintenance of an internal state away from the changes of the world.

This creates a path of least resistance to keep the consistency of an inner world that allows them to stay in touch with themselves and the universe on their terms. This usually requires minimizing certain needs that would be too tense to engage with directly and delaying reactions to sustain inner harmony.

Key Qualities

  • — Withdrawing from the Gut center’s need for presence.
  • — Perceiving the subtleties and "vibes" of relationships and unspoken characteristics.
  • — Erasing the self’s need for boundaries in favor of a more holistic perception.
  • — Feeling boundaries expand and shrink based on the qualities of changes that penetrate being.
  • — Maintaining instinctual desirability by becoming perfect mirrors to partners.

The Shadow Will

The core issue of 9 is dissociation from commitment to existence within their own beliefs and needs. While they let others have their way to reach harmony, this causes a shadow aspect of willfulness (the -3 line), leading to grudges or pent-up aggression.

They have a bipolar relationship with being noticed: deeply desiring connection, yet oversensitive to being called-out or challenged, which triggers a retreat into the inner world.

9w8

Identified with being in control of their experience. Rejects the need to move or alter their basic state. Like a snapping turtle descending into its shell; non-verbal, unmoved, and reactive.

9w1

Identified with being righteousness. Dissociates to process anger through justifications and evaluation. receipt-based mentality and exchange of energy. More calculating and philosophical.

The Narrative

The individualist split between the relaxed state of undisturbed existence and the shocks of reality.

Capable of seeing themselves in everyone naturally, yet sometimes blaming others for a lack of action on their own part due to self-erasure in moments that disrupt homeostasis.

Deep Dive

The Observant Fixation

"Interested in pure insight without obligation for Truth or communication."

The Split of Type Five

The 5 is characterized by a removal of the complexity of irrational aspects of being and an over-clarification of the conceptual source of its origin. They are often detached and uninvolved with life and their own basic needs, focusing instead on skills, methods, and systems.

Specific Fixations
  • — Focuses on specifics and details, struggling with generalities.
  • — Marked intensity in the eyes and facial muscles paired with body disconnection.
  • — Cynical about new perspectives, assuming others can't comprehend their complexity.
  • — Interested in deepening observation detached from all needs and attachments.

5w6

Movement toward the world and larger systems. Awareness of methodologies and relationships. Curiosity leans into sciences.

5w4

Movement away from the world. Dissatisfaction with the material world coupled with obsession on dissecting "essence."

Core Vice

The Vice of Neglect

Automation of existence through methods, to covertly take control and gain leverage over the world without involvement.
